Pricing Information & Best Practices
Service Offerings

Service offerings are the main services you provide to your customers. Each service can have:

  • A unique name and description
  • Fixed or free pricing
  • Duration in minutes
  • Custom icon and color for branding
  • Active/inactive status
Service Items

Service items are additional components that can be added to your services. They support:

  • Fixed Price: A set dollar amount
  • Percentage: A percentage of the base service price
  • Hourly Rate: Charged per hour
  • Per Unit: Charged per quantity
  • Free: No additional charge
Pricing Strategy Tips
  • Research competitor pricing in your area
  • Consider your costs and desired profit margin
  • Offer package deals for better value
  • Adjust prices seasonally based on demand
  • Highlight your unique value proposition
